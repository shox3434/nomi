Toyota Camry Details

The Toyota Camry is a mid-size car manufactured by Toyota. It is known for its reliability, comfort, and fuel efficiency. The Camry has been one of the best-selling cars in the United States for many years.

Specifications:

  • Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder or 3.5L V6
  • Horsepower: 203-301 hp
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 28 city / 39 highway mpg
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
  • Seating Capacity: 5 passengers
